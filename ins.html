This is a memory game. You shall win by obtaining the highest score. Earn points by correctly matching calls with their content. You will have 10 seconds to memorize the content of each cell.

After 10 seconds, the cards will flip and a question card will pop up

You will then match the contents of the question card with the corresponding cell. Correct match = 500 points. Incorrect match = 0 points.

You can monitor your score evolution on the screen as you play.
